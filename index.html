Thank you for making these past two months some of the happiest moments of my life. Thank you for being my girlfriend, my best friend, and someone who makes my life so much better just by being in it.

I hope that years from now we'll look back at this and smile, knowing that 2 June was only the beginning of our story. I hope we remember our first date, our spot, all the laughs, all the memories, and everything that brought us here.

Happy two months, my beautiful girl.

I love you more than these words could ever explain.

Forever yours,
